Why DOSPRN?

Nowadays, many laser and inkjet printers have powerful features but cannot print simple text accurately. Besides, some inexpensive printers (the so-called win-printers) can not print in text mode (dos print or dosprinter mode) at all.

DOSPRN helps to extend the life of old DOS programs. This program allows you to print reports from a good old DOS program into new printers irrespective of their types (Yes, USB and network printers, print-servers, and PDF-printers too!)

DOSPRIN will also be useful if a DOS program is in your native language. If your printer does not support your language in text mode, DOSPRN will be a good helper.
